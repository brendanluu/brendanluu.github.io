DAY 2 - Brendan on Jan 2, 2017

There's a research team at St. Christopher's Hospital for Children that wants to adapt my SupermanVR experience to help them move forward with a study on virtual reality usage during burn dressing changes. The idea is that VR will distract the patient from pain and the snowy environment will make them feel cooler.

Today, I updated my project to Unity 5.5, created a snowy particle system and changed the environment to a low-poly winter environment I found in the Asset Store.

I'm going to demo my app to the research team sometime this month.
